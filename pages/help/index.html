Popular

What is Rep-Link?

Rep-Link connects freelance sales reps with businesses using fixed or milestone commissions (no hourly).

How does Rep-Link work?

Businesses post sales opportunities with clear commission structures. Sales reps browse opportunities, submit proposals, and get matched with businesses.

How do I get paid?

Rep-Link uses milestone-based payments with escrow protection. You get paid when milestones are completed and approved.

What is the Rep-Link Success Score (RSS)?

The RSS is a rating system that helps businesses identify top-performing sales reps based on their track record.

How secure are payments?

All payments are processed through secure escrow systems with milestone-based releases and fraud protection.

How do I contact support?

You can contact our support team through the Help Center, email, or live chat during business hours.
