How It Works

Simple steps to connect and succeed on Rep-Link

1

Businesses Post Opportunities

Companies create detailed sales opportunities with clear commission structures, requirements, and project briefs.

  • Fixed price, milestone-based, or hourly projects
  • Detailed project requirements and expectations
  • Secure escrow system for payments
2

Sales Reps Apply

Qualified sales professionals browse opportunities, submit proposals with cover letters, and showcase their expertise.

  • Search and filter opportunities by category, location, and type
  • Submit detailed proposals with experience and approach
  • Build reputation through successful projects
3

Everyone Gets Paid

Secure milestone-based payments ensure both parties are protected. Funds are held in escrow and released upon completion.

  • 14-day auto-release system for completed milestones
  • Built-in dispute resolution process
  • Transparent fee structure (3% for businesses, 5% for reps)

Commission Models

Fixed Price

Pay a fixed amount upon successful completion of the entire project.

One-time payment

Best for: Well-defined projects with clear deliverables

Milestone-based

Break the project into milestones and pay for each completed deliverable.

Pay per deliverable

Best for: Long-term projects with multiple phases

Dispute Resolution

Reputation System
